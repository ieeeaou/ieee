To renew your membership, follow these steps:

Steps

  • Go to IEEE Renew website to start your membership renewal.
  • Sign in to your account.
  • Check if the "IEEE Membership" option in your cart that display on the right side. if not, add it.
  • Click continue. . .
  • When reaching the payment portal, ensure your status is set as an "Undergraduate" student.
  • Double-check that the final checkout amount is $14.00 before proceeding.
  • Reach out to our Chairperson Shaymaa Jawish to complete the registration process.
