About IEEE

IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. With members spanning across various technical fields, IEEE works to promote innovation, educate the public, and foster growth in domains like electrical engineering, computer science, and telecommunications.

IEEE History

Founded in 1963, IEEE was formed from the merging of the American Institute of Electrical Engineers (AIEE) and the Institute of Radio Engineers (IRE). Since then, IEEE has grown to become a global leader in technology and engineering, supporting numerous conferences, journals, and technical standards that shape industries worldwide.
